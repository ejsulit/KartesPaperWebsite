About Us

The Kartes Paper Company is a paper supply company in Metro Manila, Philippines providing prime and swift service to its clients in the Region, carrying paper packages in and out of the Taguig city area. The Kartes Paper Company is one of the paper supply companies situated in the Philippines, delivering paper sent from the manufacturer to the different destinations here in the National Capital Region (NCR). The company will receive the packages from the manufacturer in the Philippines and they will deliver it to the authorized representative that they transact with. Its main office in the Philippines, is located at Lower Bicutan, Taguig City.
